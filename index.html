About Me

I am a motivated, self driven worker who thrives under pressure and completing tasks on time with precision. I have had an affinity for computers ever since I first laid my fingers on a keyboard. Graduating from an avid gamer, self taught builder, and security enthuasiast, computers have always peeked my interest.


I am 32 years young, a father of 3 amazing kids, and have the worlds greatest girlfriend. When not at work or playing with my kids, I'm in front of a screen and keyboard. Over the years I have built various computers from scratch. I have troubleshooted PC problems for family ranging from a simple restart to editing registry files. My passion for cyber security fuels my decision to choose a career in web developement. I hope to one day obtain my CEH as well as other certifications. The phrase "Google it" is so often and widely used. The web directly links your work and creativity to the world at incredible speed and accessibility. Possessing the skills to reverse engineer a website has huge advantages when deciphering (un)secure code. I wanted to be apart of the World Wide Web and see my work after someone has said "Google it."
